About

Oct 23rd, 2013

Welcome to this far off spot in the very wide world of the web. Since you made the trip all the way out here —perhaps an errant click on page 56,354 of some search results— I might as well tell you why I’m here.

I’m learning Ruby and Rails. This is I having a private conversation about that in a public place. Not dissimilar from a disheveled looking gentleman mumbling to himself on the street corner. Maybe he’s just learning code. Maybe. But no talk of government conspiracy and the impending reckoning here. I’m going to keep some handy notes and observations on developing with Ruby and Rails, dig into subjects that interest me, and repeat what I’ve found in other places just so I can remember what I’ve found. Feel free to eavesdrop.
